Our Story
Born on the
Streets of Lagos

Àṣà Kitchen started as a small buka stall in Surulere. Six years later we moved to Victoria Island — not to change who we are, but to prove that Nigerian street food belongs everywhere. We cook the same way we always have. Firewood. Palm oil. Time. No shortcuts.

Ingredients sourced from Nigerian farmers weekly
Every soup cooked fresh daily — no reheating
Firewood jollof, not gas. Always.
